Public SpeakingAs the quote states, is the art of diluting a two-minute idea with a two-hour vocabulary." by John F. Kennedy. It involves taking a concise idea that could be communicated in just a few minutes and expanding it into a longer speech. The general purpose of speech is to inform, inspire, persuade and entertain. Elements of speech include: ethos, pathos and logos. By applying ethos, my introduction should capture my audience. I need to apply pathos to evoke emotions and influence perception and logos for logic. When I find myself in the role of a presenter, I will follow these steps to introduce a person:

  2. R: Relevant Background
  3. E: Expertise
  4. S: Significant achievements shared
  5. N: Noteworthy qualities emphasized
  6. T: Thankful conclusion
